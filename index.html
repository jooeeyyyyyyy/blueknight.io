See BlueKnight in Action

A closer look at the tools that power your M&A workflow.

Instantly Uncover Comparable Transactions with AI

Our AI Configurator puts you in direct control of the search algorithm. Go beyond standard filters and tell our AI precisely what matters for your analysis. Assign specific weighting factors to different criteria and our AI then evaluates and ranks comparable deals based on your custom model, delivering a truly tailored set of results that reflect the specific context of your transaction. This unparalleled control ensures more accurate valuations and highly relevant strategic insights

Unlock Data-Driven Buyer Insights

Move beyond intuition and make decisions based on empirical evidence. Our platform transforms billions of data points into a clear, actionable strategy. Each potential buyer is presented with a quantitative Match Rank, providing a precise metric of strategic fit. This score, combined with crucial data on M&A track records and financials, delivers a prioritized list of targets backed by hard data. Our data-driven findings empower you to build a more effective and defensible outreach strategy, focusing your efforts on prospects with the highest probability of engagement.

Interrogate Your Results in the AI Playgrounds

Interact with our platform like you would with an expert analyst. Our unique AI Playground is where your analysis truly comes to life. After generating an initial list of buyers, you can select specific companies and move them into this interactive space. Use the Playground's powerful conversational interface to ask direct, follow-up questions about the selected businesses.This chat-based feature allows you to instantly get the deep context and validation you need, turning a static list into a dynamic research session and helping you build a more robust, data-backed strategy
