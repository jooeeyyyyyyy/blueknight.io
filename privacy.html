Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

Privacy Statement

At BlueKnight (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Statement outlines how we collect, process, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or engage with our services, in accordance with the UK Data Protection Act 2018 and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Collection and Use of Personal Data

We may collect personal data from you when you interact with our website or use our services. This data may include your name, contact information (such as email address and phone number), company details, and any information you provide during the registration or engagement process. We use this data for purposes including but not limited to:

  • Facilitating effective communication
  • Conducting due diligence
  • Enabling matching processes for vendors and potential buyers

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, as well as to comply with legal obligations.

Data Sharing and Transfer

We may share your personal data with potential buyers and vendors to facilitate the matching process. We engage third-party service providers to support our platform’s operation, and they may have access to personal data under contractual obligations aligned with data protection laws.

Data Security

We employ appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or alterations. While we take steps to secure your data, it’s important to understand that no data transmission over the internet or storage system is entirely secure.

Your Rights

You have certain rights over your personal data, including the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. You may also have the right to object or restrict certain processing activities. To exercise these rights or for inquiries about your personal data, please contact us at info@blueknight.io.

Changes to this Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We encourage you to review this statement regularly to stay informed about how we handle your personal data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Statement or the handling of your personal data, please contact us at:

info@blueknight.io

We will respond promptly to address your inquiries and ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws.
